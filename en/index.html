Who am I?

I am Sam. I do improv and teach improv, like to go for a run from time to time. I sometimes draw ugly doodles and talk casually about all sorts of intellectual topics of which I am absolutely not an expert.

I partook in conferences and wrote research and popularization articles during my undergrads. I have also been a tutor for a bit, teaching first year students. I left after my master's thesis to explore the professional world away from academia. To me, math and physics are a language to dissect and explain the world, and as any other language, it feels quite boring to be speaking it alone.

I am generally interested in solving complex problems, societal issues and popularization. I particularly like how images convey moods and information. That is a reason why I joined the world of Animation/VFX after my education in physics.
