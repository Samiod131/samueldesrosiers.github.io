Who am I?



I am quite a regular dude. I do improv, like to go for a run from time to time and own a pet hedgehob named Billy-Bob. I sometimes draw ugly doodles and talk casually about all sorts of intellectual topics of which I am absolutely not an expert.


I partook in conferences and wrote research and popularization articles during my undergrads. I have also been a tutor for a bit, teaching first year student. I left after my master thesis to explore the professionnal world away from academia. To me math and physics are a language to dissect and explain the world, and as any other language, it feels quite boring to be speaking it alone.


I am generally interested in solving (or trying to solve) complex problems, math, programming, societal issues and popularization. I particularly like how images convey moods and information. That is a reason why I joined the world of Animation/VFX after my education in physics.
